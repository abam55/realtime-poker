📖 Texas Hold'em Rules

🎯 Objective

Win chips by making the best 5-card hand, or by making all opponents fold before showdown.

🃏 The Deal

Each player gets 2 private cards. Then 5 community cards are revealed: Flop (3), Turn (1), River (1).

💰 Blinds

Small Blind ($10) and Big Blind ($20) are posted automatically each hand and rotate clockwise.

🏆 Winning

Best hand at showdown wins. Last player standing when all others fold also wins — no cards needed!

⚡ Betting Actions
🗑️
Fold
Give up hand, lose bets
✋

Pass (if no bet)
📞

Match current bet
📈

Increase bet with slider
💥 All-In

If you bet all your chips you're all-in — you can still win but can't bet further. If all active players are all-in, the remaining community cards are dealt at once and hands are revealed immediately.

🎰 Blackjack Rules

🎯 Objective

Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over. You play against the dealer only — other players don't affect your hand.

🃏 Card Values

2 – 10 = face value
J, Q, K = 10
Ace = 11 or 1 (auto, whichever avoids bust)

💰 Betting

Bet before each hand. Min: $5 — Max: half your chips. You have 20 seconds to bet; timeout auto-places the minimum.

🏆 Payouts

Blackjack (Ace + 10) → +150%
Win → +100% (1:1)
Push (tie) → bet returned
Lose / Bust → bet lost

⚡ Player Actions
🃏
Hit
Take another card
✋
Stand
Keep current hand, end turn
×2
Double Down
Double bet, receive exactly one card. First 2 cards only.
✂️
Split
Pair only. Creates 2 hands, doubles your bet. No re-split.
🤖 Dealer Rules

• Dealer's second card is hidden until all players finish.
• Dealer must hit on 16 or below and on Soft 17 (Ace + 6).
• Dealer stands on hard 17 or above.
• If the dealer busts, all non-busted players win.
• Equal totals = dealer wins — ties go to the house. Only Blackjack vs Blackjack is a push.

💡 Quick Tips

• Blackjack = Ace + any 10-value card on first two cards only.
• Split Aces get only one card each — no further hits.
• A Blackjack on a split hand pays 1:1 (not 3:2) — it's just a 21.
• Winning hands count toward your level XP — beat the dealer to level up faster!

🃏 Welcome to Poker!

📖 How Texas Hold'em Works

Each player gets 2 private cards. Then 5 community cards are revealed in stages: the Flop (3 cards), Turn (1), and River (1). Make the best 5-card hand from your 2 cards + any of the 5 community cards.

Blinds: Small Blind ($10) + Big Blind ($20) are posted automatically each hand. Action starts left of the Big Blind.

Actions: Check (pass if no bet) · Call (match bet) · Raise (increase bet) · Fold (give up hand)

Win by: Having the best hand at showdown, or making everyone else fold.
